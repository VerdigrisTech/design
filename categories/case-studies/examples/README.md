## Provenance discipline

See `../../slides/examples/README.md` for the full anonymization checklist
and DRAFT-marker requirement. The same rules apply to case studies.

**Specific to case studies: the anchor metric.** Case studies live or die
on the single bolded outcome. When the source engagement is Lightfield-
derived and the published anchor metric has not yet been GTM-approved for
external use, leave the anchor as `[pending GTM]` rather than fabricate a
plausible-looking figure. A bracketed placeholder reads as honest while
preserving the slide structure for future fill-in. A plausible-looking
fabricated number reads as a real claim and creates downstream legal and
trust risk.
