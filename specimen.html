Brand Tone

Four Pillars

Precision
Exact, specific, and data-driven. No vagueness or hand-waving.
Visual: tight letter-spacing (-0.02em), tabular numbers, mono for data
Masterful
Expert, authoritative, and deeply knowledgeable. Command of the domain.
Visual: Inter's high legibility, 700-weight headings, confident hierarchy
Refined
Polished, elegant, and thoughtfully crafted. No rough edges.
Visual: generous whitespace, zinc-tinted neutrals, 0.625rem radius
Pioneering
Forward-thinking, innovative, and leading the industry.
Visual: OKLch color space, perceptual gradient interpolation, brand hue loop

Color Usage

Palette Semantics

Each palette region maps to a content category. AI agents and evaluators use this to select contextually appropriate colors.

Trust
Technology & Platform
Data quality, platform capabilities, primary brand expression. The anchor of the Verdigris identity.
Depth
Intelligence & Premium
AI features, enterprise sections, dark hero backgrounds. The darkest palette region — backgrounds only, not text.
Energy
Detection & Alerting
Fault detection, real-time monitoring, anomaly indicators. High chroma, demands attention.
Warmth
People & Action
Contact, customer stories, CTA hover shifts. Warm tones that invite human connection.
Results
Metrics & Outcomes
ROI, stranded capacity, financial outcomes. Bright tones (L=0.79–0.87) for proof points. Dark backgrounds only for text.
Growth
Sustainability & Recovery
M&V results, capacity recovery, environmental impact. Green-teal tones closing the loop back to brand.
